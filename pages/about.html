Meet Angelia Dubois

I am a professional photographer based in the Netherlands with a passion for capturing timeless moments. My journey began over a decade ago, and since then, I have been dedicated to the art of photography, specializing in portraits, family shoots, and fashion editorials.

Every photograph I take tells a story. My goal is to create authentic, emotional, and timeless images that you will cherish forever. Whether it's a candid moment or a carefully crafted portrait, I am committed to bringing out the best in every shot.
